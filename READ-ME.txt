3. DIRECTORY & FILE STRUCTURE
-----------------------------
All web pages are structured under a clean root hierarchy for seamless 
hosting deployment (e.g., cPanel public_html):

├── index.html            --> Core Homepage (Featuring Owl-Carousel banners)
├── about.html            --> Vision, Mission & Core Values
├── classes.html          --> Foundational Playgroup, Nursery, & KG Programs
├── team.html             --> Profiles of Mentors and Educators
├── appointment.html      --> Campus Tour Booking & Lead Capture Form
├── contact.html          --> Address Mapping & Inbound Communications
├── call-to-action.html   --> Conversion Landing Page Optimization
├── privacy.html          --> Legal Privacy Policy Statements
├── terms.html            --> Terms & Conditions Guidelines
├── copyright.html        --> Intellectual Property Protections
├── robots.txt            --> Search Engine Crawler Configurations
└── sitemap.xml           --> Search Engine Indexation Roadmap

Supporting Asset Folders:
├── css/                  --> Stylesheets (Bootstrap 5 customized)
├── js/                   --> Frontend Frameworks & i18n Translation Files
├── img/                  --> Graphic Assets, Icons, and Carousels
└── lib/                  --> Third-party plugins (Animate, Wow, OwlCarousel)
